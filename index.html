Technologies We Use for Offline AI

Our curriculum uses lightweight models and efficient frameworks that run well on consumer hardware:

  • Lightweight Libraries: Scikit-learn, Transformers.js - minimal resource requirements
  • Optimization Techniques: Quantization, pruning, and distillation to reduce model size
